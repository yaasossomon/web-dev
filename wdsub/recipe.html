My Famous Grilled Chesse Sandwhich

    Prep Work And Ingredients

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. 4 pieces of bread
  3. shreded cheader cheese
  4. oven pan

    • Directions

    1. First you take the oven pan out from the oven
    2. Second you put the 4 pieces of bread on the oven pan
    3. Thired you put the shredded chedear cheese on the bread
    4. Then you put take two of the pieces of bread and put them on top of the other two pices of bread (nothing should be on top of the bread)
    5. You then put the bread in the oven (and watch the cheese melt
    6. take out the pan and put the grilled chesse sandwich on the plate
    7. enjoy
